ActiveAlpha Careless Whisper

Careless Whisper, named after George Michael's hit song, is a search engine that searches indexes of the internet and threw sentiment analysis can calclate the general opinion of a search.

Careless Whisper was created in August of 2016 at AngelHack Boston 2016 as a 24-hour team project. Afterwards, I took it on to myself to rewrite the unorganized code making it more efficent.
